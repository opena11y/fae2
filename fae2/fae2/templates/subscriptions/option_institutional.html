Institutional Subscription Level

  • The Institutional version allows users to use their InCommon credentials to authenticate to FAE.
  • InCommon is used by most higher educational institutions as a way to provide single sign-on authentication.
  • Institutional subscriptions can create an unlimited number of accounts for faculty, staff and students at their institution.
  • Contact the administrator to setup an Instituional Subscription or to discuss your institutional licensing needs.
